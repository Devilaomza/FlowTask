# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Developers, engineers, and tech-focused professionals looking for a high-efficiency, clutter-free task management experience.

## Product Purpose
FlowTask is a minimalist, premium personal workspace designed to organize tasks with maximum speed, clarity, and zero distractions.

## Brand Personality
High-efficiency, professional, dark-mode first, expert control, focused.

## Anti-references
Avoid SaaS clichés (e.g., side-stripe card highlights, tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows, generic same-sized card grids, gradient text). Avoid cream/sand/beige default AI background tones.

## Design Principles
- **Utility and Focus**: The design must prioritize readability and ease of interacting with tasks above decoration.
- **Quiet Elegance**: Use restrained styling, low-contrast subtle borders, and intentional spacing rather than flashy borders or heavy cards.
- **Responsive Fluidity**: Ensure layouts adapt gracefully to any screen width, with text wrap balanced and no overflow.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Maintain high contrast (>= 4.5:1) for text, support reduced motion animations, and ensure clean focus states for keyboard navigation.
